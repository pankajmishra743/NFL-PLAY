About

This interactive chart attempts to visually show all the Team Statistics for years 2009 - 2019.

This interactive graph shows comparisons of following over a Season by the Teams:

  • Pass Attempts
  • Pass Completions
  • Pass Touchdowns
  • Rush Attempts
  • Rush Touchdowns
  • First Downs
  • Number of Sacks
  • Pass Interceptions
  • Fumbles
  • Fumbles Lost
  • Turnovers
  • Penalties

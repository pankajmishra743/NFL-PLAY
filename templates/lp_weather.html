These Bars show Home vs. Away team performance correlation with environmental variables, which are read year wise, from 2002 to 2018. Graphs are drawn using Charts.js. Home team has overall advantage as seen aggregated over the years. Different weather conditions do not seem to affect team winning statistics over the years to establish any kind of correlation here. It is apparent we need to study other factors like team composition, ground conditions, team-leadership, home crowd enthusiasm etc. to establish concrete causal relationship between different parameters and Home-wins, which render advantage to Home team under every weather condition.
